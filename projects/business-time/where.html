Bucket List Travel

Places to go:

While we are able to book travel to anywhere of your choosing, below are our top Bucket List picks:

ICELAND - Iceland is a scarcely populated arctic island with many wild, unpolluted places to see the Northern lights. When you're not up late enjoying the lights, there are plenty of beautiful places to explore along the countryside.

CHINA - If you are looking for a trip that is exotic, packed full of new sights and experiences, stimulating and over all different, China is the place to go. The Great Wall of China is just one of the magnificent sights of the trip.

PERU - Head to South America to the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu and experience the breathtaking sights and structures of this ancient land. While there will be other stops on the trip, Machu Picchu is sure to be the highlight!

Brazil - Treehouse lodging is the ultimate way to experience the beauty of the Amazon. You’ll never forget the experience of watching monkeys and birds at eye level as every morning you’ll wake up to a safari of animal life before you even get out of bed.

AFRICA - Imagine floating in a hot air balloon over the beautiful safari's of Africa, offering a unique perspective, great viewing and photographic opportunities of the amaizing scenery. When not floating in the air, there will be plenty to explore on ground.

CANADA - Staying in Newfoundland is beautiful in and of itself, but imagine the experience of taking an expedition out to the wreckage of the infamous Titanic. We will take you on a deep dive down to the ship, a sight only few have seen.
